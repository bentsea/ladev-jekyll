LouisiANIME May 15-17, 2015, Lafayette, Louisiana!

LouisiANIME 2015, May 15-17 in Lafayette, Louisiana

We are so excited to announce our dates and location for 2015! We are ramping up faster than ever this year by simultaneously launching pre-registration, artist alley registration, panel submissions for programming, hotel reservation information, and launching our new site for 2015. LouisiANIME is coming together at faster pace than ever and we are looking forward to seeing everyone there again this year.

We have moved to the Ramada Lafayette Convention Center with room nights starting at 79$ from May 15 - 17

This year we are keeping our early registration at only 35$ which will be available until Monday, April 13

Panel submission will remain open until Sunday, April 5th at which time we will begin finalizing the schedule

Be sure to check us out on Facebook for a more personal response to your questions and comments, but you can comment directly onto news updates as well.

We Moved!Checkout our new venue.

We're excited to announce our new venue for this year! We'll be at the Ramada Lafayette Convention center. This beautiful venue has wide open space with rooms that open directly into the convention space. We are also excited to announce that there is an indoor pool, pet friendly hotel rooms, complimentary parking for all guests, and a landscaped exterior for gorgeous photo opportunities. And we are especially excited to announce that the convention center is designed so that there should be no elevator crowding or congestion!

Vist our Location Page for more detail.

Steampunk Soiree

Come join us again for our annual Steampunk Soiree. Every year we do things a little bit differently, and this year is no exception. The Soiree consists of a combination of steampunk related instructional programming followed by a dance with thematically appropriate music. The Soiree is welcome to all, not just those in Steampunk attire.
